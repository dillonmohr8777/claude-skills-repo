Why This Visual Direction Fits
The Hope Wellness Center offers personalized mental health care rooted in compassion, trust, and real
expertise. Your homepage leads with a warm promise of comfort, peace of mind, and hope for the
community. Every illustration in this refreshed kit was chosen to carry that same gentle, human, and
reassuring tone.
Your practice serves patients through telehealth across Massachusetts, Rhode Island, New York, Colorado,
and Arizona. Flat, friendly vector illustrations travel beautifully across your website, social feeds, paid ads,
and printed handouts. They keep each scene calm and approachable while avoiding the staged feeling
that stock photography often brings.
Your logo pairs a navy brain with green leaves, a thoughtful blend of the mind and natural growth. This set
leans on that exact navy and green family, with soft blue backgrounds that create breathing room. That
consistency makes the whole collection read as one cohesive, recognizable brand system.
This refreshed direction leans into everyday wellness through movement, mindfulness, rest, and
connection. These illustrations show the real habits that support mental health between appointments
and across the week. Together they give your team a flexible, on brand kit ready for many pages and
campaigns.

Quick Audit
Your site has a strong foundation with clear service pages across medication management,
ADHD, depression, anxiety, Suboxone and MAT, therapy, and psychiatric evaluations. Care runs
through telehealth with licenses across five states, which gives you a powerful search angle well
beyond Rhode Island alone.

The largest opportunity sits with the homepage headline and a more even location strategy. The
improvements below build trust, capture more search intent, and turn your full footprint into real page
level growth.
Priority Improvements
1
Refresh the contact page copy
The contact page currently shows a leftover internal note inside the SMS consent section. Swapping
that line for polished final wording will lift trust on a key conversion page right away.
QUICK WIN · HIGH TRUST IMPACT
2
Strengthen the homepage headline
The homepage H1 reads as a warm tagline today, which leaves valuable search intent available. A
keyword led headline captures far more intent while the page already lists the right services.
Telehealth Psychiatry, Therapy & Medication Management in RI, MA, NY, CO & AZ
3
Build dedicated state and location pages
Your footer names five states, while most location content still centers on Rhode Island and Boston.
Dedicated pages for each state would capture high intent searches across your full footprint while
keeping the telehealth focus clear.
Online Psychiatry in Rhode Island  ·  Medication Management in Rhode Island  ·  Online Psychiatry in
Massachusetts  ·  Telehealth Psychiatry in New York  ·  Telehealth Psychiatry in Colorado  ·  Telehealth
Psychiatry in Arizona
4
Tidy a few service page details
A couple of service pages carry lines that belong elsewhere, such as a group therapy mention inside
ADHD content. A quick editorial pass aligns each page with its own service and call to action.
5
Add clinician bylines and reviewed by lines
A clinician byline and a reviewed by line on medical content build strong authority with readers and
search engines. Larger competitors already show clinically reviewed content across broad service
pages, and this trust signal carries real weight for healthcare search.
