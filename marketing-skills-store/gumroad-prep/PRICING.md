## Pricing rationale

- **$497 mega-pack** sits below the psychological $500 threshold + below $499 (a common SaaS pricing trick that makes us look like a "fairly-priced premium product" rather than "cheap").
- **$179 bundles** is just under $199, the next typical anchor; saves ~30% vs à la carte.
- **$29 standard skills** are above the $19-25 "impulse buy" tier, signaling production-grade vs. template-like.
- **$49 hero skills** capture buyers who want one specific result (e.g., the real-estate vertical) without committing to a bundle.

## Bundle math (for the sales page)

- Per-skill à la carte (avg): ~$30
- 6 bundles × $179 = $1,074
- À la carte all 30 skills: ~$870 (skills are mostly $29 with a few at $39-49)
- **Mega-pack at $497 saves $577 vs. all six bundles, $373 vs. à la carte all-30.**
