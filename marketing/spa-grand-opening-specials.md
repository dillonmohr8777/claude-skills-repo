# Spa Grand Opening — Specials, Promotions & Giveaways

A ready-to-launch promotional plan for the spa Grand Opening. Includes retail offers, service specials, raffle giveaways for social media and the in-person event, plus suggested social copy and an operations checklist.

---

## 1. Headline Grand Opening Offers

| Offer | Details | Goal |
|-------|---------|------|
| **Grand Opening Week — 20% Off All Services** | Valid the first 7 days. Applies to any single service booked and completed during opening week. | Drive first-visit traffic |
| **Founding Member Membership** | First 50 members lock in a discounted monthly rate for life (e.g. $89/mo for monthly facial or massage). Includes member-only pricing on retail. | Build recurring revenue |
| **Bring-a-Friend Duo** | Book any two services together and save 15% on both. | Word-of-mouth & social proof |
| **$25 Welcome Credit** | Anyone who books their first appointment during opening month receives a $25 credit toward a future service. | Repeat visits |

---

## 2. Service Specials

### Signature Service Packages
- **"Glow & Go" Express Facial** — 30 min, intro price (e.g. $59, reg. $85). Perfect for first-timers.
- **Total Reset Package** — 60-min massage + 60-min facial bundle at 20% off combined price.
- **Couples Retreat** — Side-by-side massages with a complimentary glass of sparkling cider or champagne. Add aromatherapy free during opening month.
- **Self-Care Sunday** — Sunday-only: book any service and receive a free 15-min add-on (scalp massage, hand treatment, or eye treatment).

### Add-On Specials (Free or Discounted During Opening)
- Complimentary aromatherapy upgrade with any massage.
- $10 LED light therapy add-on (reg. $30) with any facial.
- Free paraffin hand treatment with any 60+ minute service.

### Package & Series Pre-Sale
- **Buy 5, Get 1 Free** on any single service type (facials, massages, etc.) purchased as a pre-paid series during opening month.

---

## 3. Retail Promotions

- **20% Off All Retail** during Grand Opening week.
- **Free Gift With Purchase** — Spend $75+ on retail and receive a curated sample/travel kit (cleanser, moisturizer, SPF samples).
- **Buy One, Gift One** — Buy a full-size product, get a travel size of the same line at 50% off (great for gifting).
- **Retail + Service Bundle** — 15% off retail when purchased the same day as a service.
- **Gift Card Bonus** — Buy a $100 gift card, get an extra $20 bonus card (drives holiday/future revenue and new-client referrals).

---

## 4. Giveaways & Raffles

### A. Social Media Raffle (Run 1–2 weeks BEFORE opening to build buzz)
**Grand Prize: "Ultimate Spa Day" ($300+ value)**
- 60-min signature massage
- 60-min signature facial
- Retail gift bag
- Glass of bubbly / refreshments

**How to enter (pick the platform rules that fit):**
1. Follow our page (@yourspa)
2. Like the giveaway post
3. Tag 2 friends in the comments
4. Bonus entry: share the post to your story and tag us

**Runner-up prizes (2–3 winners):**
- $50 service credit
- Retail gift basket
- 30-min express facial or add-on of choice

**Timing:** Announce 10–14 days out. Draw and announce the winner LIVE during the Grand Opening event (encourages attendance).

### B. In-Person Grand Opening Raffle (Day of Event)
- Every guest who checks in / books an appointment gets a raffle ticket.
- Extra tickets for: booking a future appointment on the spot, purchasing retail, or signing up for the membership/email list.
- **Prizes drawn throughout the event:**
  - Hourly door prizes: free add-ons, travel kits, $25 credits.
  - Grand prize finale: 3-month membership or a "Season of Self-Care" package (one service per month for 3 months).

### C. Spin-to-Win Wheel (Optional, fun & high-engagement)
Every guest spins once. Sample prizes:
- 10% off today's purchase
- Free add-on
- Free product sample
- $15 service credit
- Free express service upgrade
- Grand prize raffle entry

## 6. Operations Checklist

- [ ] Finalize pricing and confirm margins on all discounts.
- [ ] Set giveaway start/end dates and official rules (eligibility, age, location, "not sponsored by [platform]" disclaimer).
- [ ] Design social graphics for teaser, announcement, and urgency posts.
- [ ] Print raffle tickets, signage, and spin-wheel prize list.
- [ ] Prep gift bags / sample kits and confirm retail inventory.
- [ ] Set up booking system promo codes for each offer.
- [ ] Brief front-desk staff on offers, raffle rules, and membership pitch.
- [ ] Schedule social posts (teaser → countdown → live winner announcement → recap).
- [ ] Capture photos/video at the event for follow-up recap posts.
- [ ] Plan post-opening follow-up email to all new contacts with their $25 welcome credit.

---

*Tip: Keep discounts time-boxed to opening week/month to create urgency, and require booking or contact info to enter raffles so every promotion also grows your client list.*
